Programs

Language Arts

Through Beestar's ELA program, students will be provided with a comprehensive and structured approach to developing language and literary skills in four main areas: reading, writing, speaking and listening, and language arts.
1

First Grade

use simple words, infer from text, recall details, understand words by context, distinguish rhyming words
2

Second Grade

recall details, basic grammar usage, compose sentences, match word sounds, support conclusions, identify purpose
3

Third Grade

recall details, main idea, learn words by context, identify writing purpose, basic grammar usage, connect ideas
4

Fourth Grade

basic grammar, draw inferences, use conjunctions, use pronouns, capitalization, punctuation, draw conclusions
5

Fifth Grade

word root, use apostrophes, basic grammar, summarize text, recall detail with cues, draw inferences, distinguish fact and opinion
6

Sixth Grade

recall/locate details, writing with purpose, capitalization, punctuation, develop vocabulary through context
7

Seventh Grade

connect with conjunctions, recall/locate details, word meaning/idiom reasoning, standard grammar and usage
8

Eighth Grade

conclude main idea, standard grammar and usage, draw inferences, logical sentence structure, use verb tenses
